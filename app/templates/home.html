FraXrAi

Fracture detection using AI




What is FraXrAi?


X-rays take a trained expert to identify fractures and other issues. An AI will be used to assist in the identification of simple fractures (A complete break inside the body) and compound fractures (When bone breaches the skin) in the long bones of the legs and arms. The X-ray would be uploaded by the medical professional and scanned by the AI to insure that no fractures are missed. The X-ray would then be quickly processed and a report will be created detailing where the fractures are.
